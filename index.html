Gameplay

Develop miniature dragon city on floating islands. Gold generated by dragons can be used to buy or upgrade buildings and habitats. In addition, farms can exchange gold for food or use to evolve dragons, improving strength.

Dragon's health and strength depends on rarity. Crossbreeding allows two dragons of level 4 or higher to spawn either a hybrid or a legendary dragon. Diamonds are very important in Dragon City, which you can buy or collect by completing quests.

Arena, Tournament and Mission are different game modes. In it, one of the player's three dragons fights one of the opponent's three dragons. When entering battle, the dragon's damage can be strong or weak, depending on the dragon's elements. Tournaments also allow you to use any combination of dragons, including only one or two.
